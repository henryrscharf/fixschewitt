Overview 		My research interests are varied and changing. Most recently, I am interested in spatial and spatio-temporal modeling, including applications in animal movement and landscape genetics. I am currently working with Dr. Mevin Hooten on animal movement models for pinnipeds using satellite telemetry. Previously I worked with Dr. Ryan Elmore at the National Renewable Energy Lab in Golden, CO on methods for optimally compressing simulated wind turbine array data.
